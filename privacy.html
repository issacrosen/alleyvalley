Privacy Policy

Your privacy & your trust

Last updated:

Overview

Alley Valley ("Alley Valley," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you share with us through our website, our services, and our text-messaging (SMS) program, including the NYS SCN Food Program.

Information we collect

We may collect information you provide directly, such as your name, mobile phone number, contact details, and any information you share when you check program eligibility or sign up for text updates. We may also collect limited technical information, such as your IP address, when you submit a form on our website.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your requests and check your eligibility for the SCN Food Program;
  • To send you text messages you have opted in to receive (program updates, eligibility help, and reminders);
  • To provide and improve our care, services, and support; and
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations.

SMS / mobile messaging

When you opt in to receive text messages from Alley Valley, you consent to receive recurring marketing and informational SMS messages at the mobile number you provide. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP, and you can reply HELP or call 845-351-0300 for assistance.

Mobile information will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All of the above categories exclude text-messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

How we share information

We may share information with service providers who help us operate our website and messaging program, and only to the extent necessary to provide those services to us. We may also disclose information where required by law. We do not sell consumer information, and we do not share end-user data with third parties to sell or market their products or services.

Data security

We maintain reasonable physical, administrative, and technical safeguards designed to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure, and we review these measures on a regular basis.

Your choices

You may opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. You may also contact us to update or request removal of your information using the details below.
